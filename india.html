To cook Biryani

Marinate the Chicken: Mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chopped green chilies, spices, and salt. Marinate chicken for at least 1 hour. Prepare the Rice: Boil water with whole spices and salt. Cook rice until 70-80% done, then drain and set aside.

Layering: Sauté onions until caramelized. Reserve half for garnish. Arrange marinated chicken in a pot over onions. Add mint and cilantro. Layer partially cooked rice over chicken. Drizzle saffron milk and top with reserved onions.

Cooking: Cover and cook on low heat for 25-30 minutes until chicken is cooked through and rice is fully cooked. Serve: Gently fluff biryani with a fork. Serve hot with raita and salad.

To Cook Tandoori Chicken

First, prepare the marinade in a bowl. Then, mix together the yogurt, lemon juice and oil. Now, add all the spices and mix. Next, remove the chicken skin and cut some slits into the chicken legs. Now, add the marinade to the chicken and rub well. Finally, cover and marinate for a minimum of 1 hour before cooking. Now all that is left is to DEVOUR.

To Cook Butter Chicken

Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices, then bake until cooked. Sauté onion, ginger-garlic paste, and spices in butter and oil. Add tomato tomato puree, cream, honey, and salt. Simmer until thickened. Add cooked chicken to the sauce and simmer for a few more minutes. Garnish with cilantro leaves and serve hot with rice or naan.

To Make Kheer

Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes, then drain. In a saucepan, bring milk to a boil. Add rice and simmer on low heat, stirring occasionally, until rice is cooked and mixture thickens (about 30-40 minutes). Sweeten with sugar, add cardamom powder, and saffron strands if using. Stir in chopped nuts and simmer for a few more minutes. Serve warm or chilled, garnished with raisins and additional nuts if desired.
