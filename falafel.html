Falafel Recipe

Instructions:

  1. Place dried chickpeas in a bowl and cover with cold water. Allow to soak overnight, then drain.
  2. In a food processor, combine drained chickpeas, onion, garlic, parsley, cilantro, salt, red pepper, cumin, and baking powder. Process until blended but not pureed.
  3. Sprinkle in the flour and pulse. You want to add enough so that the dough forms a small ball and no longer sticks to your hands.
  4. Transfer mixture to a bowl, cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  5. Form the falafel mixture into small balls, about the size of a ping pong ball.
  6. Heat vegetable oil in a deep pot over medium heat until it reaches 350°F (175°C).
  7. Fry the falafel balls in batches for about 3-5 minutes, until they are golden brown and crispy on the outside.
  8. Remove falafel from oil with a slotted spoon and drain on paper towels.
  9. Serve hot falafel in pita bread with tahini sauce, chopped tomatoes, cucumbers, lettuce, and other desired toppings.
  10. Enjoy your homemade falafel!

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas
  • 1/2 large onion, roughly chopped (about 1 cup)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2-1 teaspoon dried hot red pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 4-6 tablespoons flour
  • Vegetable oil for frying
