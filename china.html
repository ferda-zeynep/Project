To Cook Kung Pao chicken

In a pot, bring chicken or vegetable broth to a simmer over medium heat. Add minced garlic, sliced ginger, and chopped green onions to the pot. Simmer for 5 minutes to infuse the broth with flavor. Add mixed vegetables and diced tofu to the pot and simmer until vegetables are tender. Season the soup with soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper according to taste. Serve hot and enjoy your comforting Chinese Soup!

To Cook Fried Rice

Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat. Add beaten eggs and scramble until cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside. In the same skillet, add a little more oil if needed. Add minced garlic and stir-fry until fragrant. Add mixed vegetables and cooked protein to the skillet and stir-fry until heated through. Add chilled cooked rice to the skillet and break up any clumps with a spatula. Stir-fry until the rice is heated through and evenly coated with the other ingredients. Pour soy sauce over the fried rice and toss to combine. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Return the scrambled eggs to the skillet and stir-fry briefly until everything is well mixed. Garnish with chopped green onions and serve hot. Enjoy your flavorful Fried Rice!
